Old Fashioned

Description

A classic drink and maybe the world's oldest cocktail. The old fashioned is perfect for the fall and winter seasons. The whiskey warms your insides with compliments of the smokey bitters and citrus notes. The old fashioned can be made in seconds and sipped for hours.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot whiskey of choice
  • 1 1.5" ice cube
  • 2 bar spoons of tap water
  • Old fashioned or orange bitters
  • 1 sugar cube
  • 1 orange

Steps

  1. Place sugar cube in rocks glass.
  2. Add 2 bar spoons of tap water and two dashes of bitters.
  3. Muddle sugar cube
  4. Swirl glass to coat the sides with the mixture.
  5. Add ice cube.
  6. Pour in shot of whiskey of choice.
  7. With a sharp knife, cut rind of orange into 3inch long by 1inch wide slice.
  8. Twist orange rind slice over glass and then swirl the slice around the lip of the glass.
  9. Stir the old fashioned with the bar spoon for 30 seconds.
  10. Enjoy next to a fire with loved ones.
